Free-spirited and eccentric, Zene is a young man who puts his faith of his fate in the roll of his trusty dice.

With the flick of a wrist, destiny is determined. Serving as captain of the grand airship Namaste, Zene and his dice

guide he and his quirky crew on a journey to retrieve the fabled Spirit Chasm; a talisman said to bring prosperity

to those who claim it. Not long into their adventures, mysterious elements begin to take an interest in the travelers

and steer their fate through trials, tribulations, and forces. Some found within the world they know, while other

entities hail from a veiled realm, unseen to their mortal eyes. Will the roll of the dice steer their destiny to fortune,

or will Zene's luck finally run out?
