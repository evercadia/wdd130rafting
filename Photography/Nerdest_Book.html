Writings of Evercadia!!

Free-spirited and eccentric, Zene is a young man who puts his faith of his fate in the roll of his trusty dice.

With the flick of a wrist, destiny is determined. Serving as captain of the grand airship Namaste, Zene and his dice

guide he and his quirky crew on a journey to retrieve the fabled Spirit Chasm; a talisman said to bring prosperity

to those who claim it. Not long into their adventures, mysterious elements begin to take an interest in the travelers

and steer their fate through trials, tribulations, and forces. Some found within the world they know, while other

entities hail from a veiled realm, unseen to their mortal eyes. Will the roll of the dice steer their destiny to fortune,

or will Zene's luck finally run out?

I love to write Poetry, I feel its one of the best ways that I am able to connect with people

To be able to share a part of yourself that you yourself didn't know even existed is very inspiring to me.

I feel inspiration is a small seed that is within us all, I love to help spark that inspiration to help cultivate it

into something greater, something deeper, something so small yet profound, that it can help shape their lives.

Poem of the Day

A proud viking were the rivers three

We crossed the stars to reach the world tree

Yggdrasil lay at the heart of lightning and snow

Truth I found, faint with a shimmering glow

I spiraled the winds till my wolfe met I

Together we forged with a sun-drawn eye

His love proved the forge to my sail

We voyaged the dawn through land, magma, and even the hail

Other spirits I bestowed upon our safe haven

One part wolfe, one part raven

Ancestor spirits call me now to another land

Our canversations we never had

but I gave to you a son, s spirit, a lad

Only a temporary momeny have I parted

Flyn will know where to seek, back to where we started

A heart of wisdon

A heart of gold

A heart of spirit

A heart so bold

We shall meet again, for I am Marigold
