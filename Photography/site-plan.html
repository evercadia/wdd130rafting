Welcome to Evercadia

The front page will contain my a photo of what I call my masterpiece, my book Evercadia. I will have a short paragraph giving the preface of Evercadia. To the right of the paragraph, will be a link to where my readers can purchase a copy of my poems or Evercadia for themselves. I will also have a short (rotating) list and example of some of my short work poems. My purpose here would to define just a sliver of inspiration to leave them wanting more. If I can inspire them, then has my goal reached it's fruition.. If I am able to max a little extra income, then that's the icing on the cake.. My true end goal is inspire the minds of others. Photography is using the creative powers of a camera to captivate and uplift others through picture. It is also used when one needs to express a message when words won't suffice

The heart of all of my artwork, my written poetry and books. I love writing and hope to be able to find readers of all ages. From page 1 to the final page, I want to have them feel uplifted, even though Evercadia is fantasy/ science fiction. I believe all (well written) books should leave you with a feeling of self that beems with positive energy. I tend to be a descriptive writter and love using imagery in my phrases and cues. Photograohy

I love photography! it symbolizes everything that is beautiful in this world. From sunsets to landscapes, to the unknowing shadow. Everthing possesses beauty in this world and I desire to bring it out. Sunsets upon the water are my favorite. I have a special love for black and white photos. It brings out the image in such a way as to give it a whole different feel altogether. I also enjoy adding light textures to paintings or pictures I already own, to give them a completly different spin on them.
