My Blog

Purpose

My purpose here is to introduce myself to an array of readers and to open up their imaginations. I love taking photography and using light to enhance its properties

Some people paint the world with colors and black ink, I paint the world with words. I want to spark the life of imagination and creating a blog will help me accomplish this.

Pictures can help create feelings when words aren't able to express them properly. I want to help people express their deep feelings through my photography.

Nerd Book Corner

Free-spirited and eccentric, Zene is a young man who puts his faith of his fate in the roll of his trusty dice.

With the flick of a wrist, destiny is determined. Serving as captain of the grand airship Namaste, Zene and his dice

guide he and his quirky crew on a journey to retrieve the fabled Spirit Chasm; a talisman said to bring prosperity

to those who claim it. Not long into their adventures, mysterious elements begin to take an interest in the travelers

and steer their fate through trials, tribulations, and forces. Some found within the world they know, while other

entities hail from a veiled realm, unseen to their mortal eyes. Will the roll of the dice steer their destiny to fortune,

or will Zene's luck finally run out?

Photo Corner

Photography of Evercadia!!

I love light! It changes every aspect in our lives, especially when it comes to art and photography

Notice my hands and how the light magnifies my wrinkles while bringing a certain beauty to the picture.

Notice how it brings to life the mask and the doll picture, it has the ability to changes the atmosphere around it

The darth Vader and Luke figurine is just one of many ways how using a smoke bomb at the bottom enhances the effect of their battle

By adding a red tint light to the painting, it almost creates a door atmosphere that the being is looking through

These are just some of the ways that I love to create artwork, I would love to here about some of your ideas...

Follow my on social media and leave your comments there, I would love to hear from you!
